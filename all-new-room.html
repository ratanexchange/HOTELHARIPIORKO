    A Comfortable Budget Abode In New Delhi

    Hari Piorko denotes 'Love to God'.  At Hotel Hari Piorko we provide a familiar feeling of home and safety. We aim to establish an inspirational landmark in the hotel industry by offering the best in town. Hotel Hari Piorko, one of the hotels near New Delhi Railway Station, stands in heart of Main Bazar (which is a renowned Market among foreigners), Paharganj, New Delhi. Hotel Hari Piorko is the symbol of Hospitality covering 4000 sq yards area with elegant interiors and 60 well decorated rooms, grand reception area and lobby. We are located at a five minutes distance from R K Ashram Metro station and New Delhi Railway station.

    The guests can choose from a variety of room options - Semi Deluxe Rooms, Deluxe Rooms, Executive Rooms, Executive Gold Rooms and Quad Rooms. These rooms are spacious and beautiful with elegant interiors & teak wood furniture designed for utmost comfort.

    Where Hospitality is Passion, Not Profession

    The guests can also enjoy a relaxing and rejuvenating Ayurvedic Massage in our Spa center. Our trained expert will make sure that you can de-stress after a long day of scouting the capital city. 

    There is energy and life in every corner of our hotel. We assure comfort for your body & soul through the relaxing ambiance of the facility.

    Our in-house diner, Hari Piorko Restaurant, offers multi-cuisine delicacies to the guests for a flavorsome treat. We provide the best fine dining experience in Paharganj. We offer both indoor and outdoor seating arrangements. So, enjoy a gastronomical experience 

    Apart from these, guests can also utilise our travel desk and money exchange facility which makes your vacation convenient at one of the best budget hotels in Delhi.
